DeltaRem is a London-based software company founded at 2017. The company is targeting to build global teams working remotely from anywhere so the cost of hiring talent is lower than its competitor companies

The production model in software development industry is changing dramatically. In the new model there is no need for software developers to work in the company offices with their colleagues, they can join to production pipeline in any location that has internet access. They can work remotely at home, in a coffee shop or a train station so that is a big evolution in the software development world. It removes borders of country for companies to hire people from any country in the world. In the past, companies had to either relocate new hires or invest in expensive overseas facilities to obtain global talent. In our modern world, the best skilled labor is available through the cloud.
